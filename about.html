About Us

Who is JY Review?

JY Review is founded by an individual that lives in Malaysia. His real name will be keeps in secret so you all can just know him as JY. JY is a university student who always travels around Malaysia and making reviews on all the things that he discover.

Objectives of JY Review?

One day, JY realizes that many people around him do not know much about Malaysia. Even his overseas friends do not understand why choose Malaysia as place of tourist. And so JY created JY Review during his free time to share his knowledge and reviews on all the things in Malaysia. This web site is also for the Malaysian local as many of them actually are still oblivious about beauty of Malaysia. Hopefully, more people will know the beauty of Malaysia and visit more places in Malaysia.

Be Part of Our Community

At first, all the contents of this web site only supplied by JY. But recently, more and more readers have been contacting JY as they introducing more good things in Malaysia such as food or traveling spot which even JY do not aware of. And so, JY Review can be known as Malaysia lovers community as many people support this web site by sharing their knowledge too. Be part of our community and increase more Malaysia lovers.
